🛠️ Tools of the Trade

Recon is the map. Exploits are the journey. Know your tools.

🔍 Recon Workflow

Start wide, go deep. These tools help you map the attack surface before diving into vulnerabilities.

  • Workflow: Subfinder → httpx → Nmap → ffuf → Burp Suite
    🐞 Bug bounty angle: This chain reveals subdomains, checks for live hosts, scans ports, fuzzes endpoints, and finally inspects requests in real-time.

🌐 Subfinder

subfinder -d target.com
  • Purpose: Finds subdomains using passive sources.
  • 🐞 Bug bounty angle: Uncover forgotten or vulnerable subdomains that expand your attack surface.

⚡ httpx

httpx -l subdomains.txt -status-code -title
  • Purpose: Probes subdomains for HTTP services and metadata.
  • 🐞 Bug bounty angle: Identify live hosts, tech stack, and potential entry points.

🔍 Nmap

nmap -sV target.com
  • Purpose: Scans ports and detects services.
  • 🐞 Bug bounty angle: Find exposed services, outdated software, or misconfigured ports.

🧪 ffuf

ffuf -u https://target.com/FUZZ -w wordlist.txt
  • Purpose: Fuzzes directories, parameters, and endpoints.
  • 🐞 Bug bounty angle: Discover hidden pages, APIs, or vulnerable parameters.

🛡️ Burp Suite

  • Purpose: Intercepts and manipulates HTTP traffic.
  • 🐞 Bug bounty angle: Modify requests, test for injections, and automate scans.

🧪 Try This:

Intercept → Modify → Forward → Analyze
